PDAI: Price-mediated-unlock Vault, with timelock backup

About This App

• Lock pDAI until price/time conditions are met.
• Deposit pDAI by sending tokens directly to the vault address.
• Withdraw once price threshold or backup unlock time is met.

• All metadata (threshold & unlock time) is stored on-chain.
• Vault addresses are stored locally for convenience.
• You can re-add vaults manually using “Add Vault Address”.
